How to build our own privacy fence?

Qizhen did not just jump in the composite fencing. It evolved from wood decking as consumer demand for ease of maintenance and other benefits grew. Over the years, Qizhen made improvement after improvement on the products.

Qizhen wood plastic composite is a full line of sustainable composite fencing products. Qizhen allows consumers, contractors and architects to design and build virtually any style of fencing system including, but not limited to privacy, ranch rail and ornamental styles. All of Natures Composites fencing systems are available in 6 colors whereas most of our competitors only offer 1 or 2 colors.

Shanghai Seven Trust Industry Co., Ltd use the composite to make the privacy fence. The materials is natural and environment, and do not have bad smell after install. And do not need paint when install, which can also save the installment time and save money.

Wood plastic composite fencing materials are pretty resilient. If it is your desire that your composite fence maintain that 'just installed' look then you will want to establish a periodic cleaning cycle of 1x or 2x per year, depending on your environment and lifestyle.
